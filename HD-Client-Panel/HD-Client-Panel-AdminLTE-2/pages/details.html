January 16, 2020 01:18 pm Reports

Top Customer Analysis gives and helps you to understand which organization has the highest number of tickets trends in the kinds of tickets they've raised and in that tickets how many resolved, unresolved and reopened.

It also provides how many tickets met with a response and resolution SLA and reported with the number of client and agent replies.

Faveo Support system provides you the different chart types to view the report:

  • Bar
  • Horizontal Bar
  • Pie
  • Doughnut

The Analytical chart is displayed for the top organizations with the tickets count.

Your top 5 customers will be displayed, based on the following metrics:

Metrics What it means
Tickets Received from the organizations In this, it displays the highest number of tickets created by the organizations through any channel.
Tickets resolved for the organization It displays the organizations which have the highest count of resolved tickets in the chart.
Tickets unresolved count of the top organization Here, the highest number of tickets that were not resolved in the top organizations are displayed. 
Tickets reopened by the organization The highest number of tickets reopened by the organization is displayed with the ticket count.
Tickets met with Response SLA and replied It shows the top organizations which have the highest count of the tickets raised and that is met Respond SLA and replied.
Tickets which are met with Resolution SLA  Here, it shows the highest number of tickets that were met with Resolution SLA displayed in the chart for the top organization.

Client Responses

(Client Interaction on the tickets)

 Displays the chart for the total number of responses added by the customer on the tickets for the top organization.
Agent Responses (Agent Interactions)   It gives an analytical chart about the agent responses on the tickets for the top organization.

You can use the filters to dig deeper and find out answers to specific questions, For example, “Only to view the Opened tickets of the organization”, etc...

A Quick Guide to view top customer analysis report:

  • ogin to Faveo Support Portal as an Agent
  • Navigate to Agent Panel > Reports > Top Customer Analysis.
  • When you select the Top Customer Analysis Report following are the analytical report’s charts are displayed.
Received Tickets:

In this, the analytical report is given on the Top organizations in which the highest tickets received.
